About Me

I am an aspiring software developer born in Taiwan who recently graduated from the University of San Francisco with a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science. I am passionate about technology and app innovations that can bring positive impact to our lives.

So far I've built an animal colony management web app, a search engine, a unix system inspector, a bill and tip splitting calculator app on the App Store, and a website featuring infographics of crimes in San Franciso. I work with JavaScript frameworks such as D3.js to create data visualizations and React/Vue.js to build websites.

Outside of the tech world and computer science, I am a sneakerhead and an avid follower of NBA Basketball. My favorite team is the Golden State Warriors.
